Frequently Asked Questions

  • Who is Responsive Design? and why is important?

    Responsive design is a web design that is adaptable to different mobile devices such as laptops, different models of cell phones and tablets. It is important because it gives a good user experience to people who visit your website and spend more time looking at your products and services.

  • Why is important Optimized code?

    If the code is not optimized, your website will be slow and people will leave your website.

  • Why should i have a website?

    People perceive a brand that has a website to be more serious and feel more confident in purchasing its products and services. Your brand will also be able to position itself in search engines such as Google or Bing and people will be able to find you with keywords.

  • What is SEO?

    SEO(Search Engine Optimization) is the process of improving the visibility of a website in the organic results of search engines, that is, the company that owns the search engine is not paid for positioning.

  • What is a SSL Certificate? and why do you need one?

    Businesses and organizations must add SSL certificates to their websites to protect and maintain the privacy and security of customer information. The SSL certificate keeps Internet connections secure and prevents criminals from reading or modifying transferred information.
