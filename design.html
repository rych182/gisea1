Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why should I have a logo?

    The logo is the first presentation of a company to consumers. If designed well, it can pique the interest of the public and make them want to discover more about the company; This first impression is your way of immediately communicating the products you sell or the niche you dominate.

  • Why should I in content for my social networks?

    Feeding your content on social networks gives you greater visibility that will not only help you create more traffic on your official site, but will also help publicize your products and/or services. Being present on social networks also increases the possibility that prospective clients will find your services more easily when searching for information about them on the networks. A well-structured plan and consistency in the support of your social networks are the basis of the success of your investment.

  • What happens if I don't have a good image on social networks?

    It does not matter if you are a restaurant, an entrepreneur or a tourist agency, it is useless to have images with poor resolution or photographs that do not convey any message on your social networks or website. To differentiate yourself and stand out from the competition, you need not only quality and original content, but also careful and eye-catching visual content to rely on. From photographs, infographics, illustrations, memes, GIFs or videos, among others, that reinforce and establish your brand image, Create corporate identity and make a good impression on customers.
